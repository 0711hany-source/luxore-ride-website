Our Vision

Our Vision

LUXORÉ RIDE was founded with the clear goal of taking limousine service to an entirely new, exclusive level.

We believe that a ride is more than just getting from point A to point B. It is your precious time. Time to work, relax, or simply enjoy the moment. With our fleet of black luxury vehicles and our personal, highly detailed service, we aim to offer our guests an unforgettable first-class feeling. We bring profound empathy and an eye for absolute perfection to every one of your journeys.

Our Expertise.

Business Tours

Booking Conditions in Detail

Flexibility & Cancellation
  • Free cancellation: Flexible up to 24 hours before the scheduled departure.
  • Short-term cancellations: For notice of less than 24 hours, the full agreed price will be charged to compensate our drivers.
Transparent Waiting Times
  • Airport (Commercial flights): Your first 60 minutes after landing are included as a free buffer.
  • General pickup locations: You enjoy a relaxed 15 minutes of free waiting time at the agreed meeting point.
  • Extended waiting time: After that, we charge a flat rate of €40 for every started half hour.
Billing & Payment
  • Payment methods: Processing is simple, secure, and convenient across all standard electronic channels.
  • Price calculation: All listed rates are gross, including the statutory value-added tax of 19%.
